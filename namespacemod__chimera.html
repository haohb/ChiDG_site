subroutine mod_chimera::detect_chimera_faces ( type(mesh_t), dimension(:), intent(inout)  mesh)

Routine for detecting Chimera faces.

Routine flags face as a Chimera face if it has an ftype==ORPHAN, indicating it is not an interior face and it has not been assigned a boundary condition.

Author
Nathan A. Wukie
Parameters
[in,out]meshArray of mesh types. One for each domain.
